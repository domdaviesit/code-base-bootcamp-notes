Semantic HTML

Structure the page with meaning. Not just a bunch of divs dividing up the content but meaningful sections. Good for accessibility/screenreaders and reading code.

A semantic element clearly describes its meaning to both the browser and the developer. Examples of non-semantic elements: div and span - Tells nothing about its content. Examples of semantic elements:

  • form
  • table
  • article
- Clearly defines its content.
